# Week 25, 2025 — Jun 16–22

> **Key Takeaway:** A week that captured AI's full range — from absurd (Gemini panicking at Pokémon) to consequential (Apple bringing LLMs offline for developers). Midjourney entering video signals the next frontier in generative media, while Google potentially cutting Scale AI ties reflects how the data labeling supply chain is becoming a geopolitical asset, not just a vendor relationship.

---

## 📰 News

- **Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash "panicked" while playing Pokémon** — Model produced erratic, incoherent responses during gameplay. More than a curiosity: raises questions about AI reliability under complex, real-time interactive conditions, and what that means for high-stakes deployments in healthcare, finance, or autonomous driving. *(Jun 2025)*
- **Google reportedly planning to cut ties with Scale AI** — Follows Meta's $14.3B Scale AI investment (W24). Signals that data labeling is becoming a strategic asset in the AI wars — cross-investment tensions are fracturing the previously collaborative vendor ecosystem. *(Jun 2025)*
- **Jensen Huang and Sam Altman reflect on AI and jobs** — Huang: AI requires "re-skilling millions"; Altman: AI will create "new kinds of jobs we haven't yet imagined." The employment question remains open, with both optimistic and cautionary readings possible. *(Jun 2025)*

## 🚀 Model / Product Launches

- **[Google Gemini 2.5 Family Expansion (DeepMind)](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/)** — Stronger multimodal capabilities, lower latency, better Workspace integration. Part of Google's push to compete with GPT-4o in AI assistant tech. *(Jun 2025)*
- **[Apple offline AI for developers (WWDC)](https://developer.apple.com/wwdc25/)** — Developers can now access Apple's on-device LLMs for privacy-first, offline intelligent apps. Part of "Apple Intelligence" strategy. No cloud dependency; Apple-scale adoption makes this significant for the entire mobile AI ecosystem. *(Jun 2025)*
- **[Midjourney V1 Video Generator](https://www.midjourney.com)** — Midjourney enters the AI video arena, rivaling Runway, Pika, and Sora. Brings Midjourney's distinctive visual style to video generation. *(Jun 2025)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- The Pokémon panic is funny until it isn't. If a frontier model becomes incoherent in a structured game environment, the reliability bar for agentic tasks in messy real-world contexts is sobering.
- Apple's offline AI move is potentially the most quietly consequential launch of the week. Billions of devices, no cloud dependency, developer access — this could make on-device AI the default for consumer applications.
- Google cutting Scale AI while Meta doubles down on it ($14.3B, W24) is the most visible sign yet that the data pipeline is becoming a battleground, not just infrastructure.
- Midjourney in video completes the generative media stack: image → video. The next 12 months will determine whether stylistic quality or temporal coherence wins the video generation race.
