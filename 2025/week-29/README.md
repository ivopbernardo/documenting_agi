# Week 29, 2025 — Jul 14–20

> **Key Takeaway:** Three major labs jointly admitted they may no longer fully understand the models they're building — a rare and significant moment of institutional honesty. In the same week, OpenAI launched a true multi-step ChatGPT Agent and Meta acquired a voice AI startup while announcing a new supercluster. The gap between what AI can do and what we understand about it is widening faster than anyone expected.

---

## 📰 News

- **OpenAI, DeepMind & Anthropic: "We may be losing the ability to interpret our models"** — Rare joint statement from the three leading AI labs warning that as models become more powerful, interpretability is falling behind. Black-box concerns now coming from the builders themselves. Positive signal for alignment research getting taken seriously. *(Jul 2025)*
- **Meta acquires Play AI — expressive voice model startup** — Meta continues building the full GenAI stack: infra, models, and now hyper-realistic voice. Complements smart glasses ambitions. Zuckerberg also announced a new AI data center supercluster coming online next year. *(Jul 2025)*
- **Google launches AI-powered business calling feature** — Voice assistant that calls businesses on your behalf, leveraging Google's Search/Maps business data. Targets business-facing GenAI and Google's massive B2B lead generation ecosystem. *(Jul 2025)*
- **Mistral launches "Deep Research" agent and voice mode for Le Chat** — Moves beyond open-weight models into enterprise tooling; directly competing with OpenAI on agentic and voice features. *(Jul 2025)*
- **HBR: Can copyright and GenAI coexist?** — Deep dive into IP, licensing, and attribution under the GenAI paradigm. Copyright laws will need fundamental rethinking; the fair-use rulings (W26) bought time, not permanence. *(Jul 2025)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- The joint labs statement on interpretability is historically significant: the builders of the most powerful AI systems are publicly acknowledging they don't fully understand them. This is the clearest call yet for alignment research to be treated as existential, not academic.
- ChatGPT Agent is the product that turns "AI assistant" into "AI employee." Booking flights and filing taxes are table-stakes; building apps autonomously is a different category of capability.
- Meta acquiring Play AI while announcing a supercluster shows a company executing a vertically integrated AI stack with rare discipline: compute → models → voice → wearables. Each piece reinforces the others.
- Mistral adding enterprise agents and voice signals that the open-weight model strategy was always a trojan horse for the full-stack enterprise play. The model is the entry point; the platform is the business.
