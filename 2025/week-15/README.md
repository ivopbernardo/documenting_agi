# Week 15, 2025 — Apr 07–12

> **Key Takeaway:** A model release week with clear strategic intent behind each launch. Meta's LLaMA 4 doubled down on "open but powerful" with a 2T parameter behemoth in the pipeline; Amazon entered the agent SDK race to break ecosystem lock-in; Anthropic priced Claude at $200/month to signal serious professional positioning. OpenAI's EU policy blueprint showed that the regulatory battle is now as important as the model race.

---

## 📰 News

- **OpenAI publishes EU economic blueprint** — Policy proposal titled "The Economic Case for AI in Europe" aimed at convincing EU policymakers to foster AI innovation rather than over-regulate. Part defense, part lobbying — signals AI companies racing to shape regulation before it's written. *(Apr 2025)*

---

## 🚀 Model / Product Launches

- **[Meta LLaMA 4](https://ai.meta.com/blog/llama-4-multimodal-intelligence/)** — New family of open-weight foundation models. Scout and Maverick variants optimized for long-context reasoning. A 2-trillion parameter model in development. Open weights with commercial use options. Meta's strongest bid to lead the "open but powerful" category. *(Apr 2025)*
- **[Amazon Nova Agent SDK](https://aws.amazon.com/nova/)** — Open-source SDK for building AI agents across platforms. Targets ecosystem lock-in of OpenAI, Microsoft, and Salesforce. Designed for interoperability, custom deployments, and developer flexibility. *(Apr 2025)*
- **[Claude Pro+ — $200/month tier (Anthropic)](https://anthropic.com)** — Premium subscription giving access to Claude 3 Opus, longer context, faster responses, and priority access during peak hours. Targets researchers, power users, and high-stakes professional use cases. *(Apr 2025)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- LLaMA 4 with a 2T parameter model in the pipeline is a direct challenge to the assumption that only closed labs can operate at frontier scale. If Meta ships it open-weight, it changes the economics of the entire industry.
- Amazon Nova's SDK play is about distribution, not model quality — AWS wants to be the infrastructure layer for AI agents the same way it became the infrastructure layer for cloud compute.
- Claude at $200/month is a deliberate signal: Anthropic is not competing for the mass consumer market, it's targeting the professional and enterprise segment where margin exists.
- OpenAI's EU blueprint is the clearest sign yet that winning the regulatory narrative is now as strategically important as winning the model benchmarks.
