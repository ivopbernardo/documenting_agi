# Week 5, 2025 — Jan 27–31

> **Key Takeaway:** Two major open-source/agentic developments redefined expectations: DeepSeek R1 proved the open-source community can match proprietary reasoning models at a fraction of the cost, while OpenAI Operator put agentic, computer-using workflows on the map. The AI race is no longer a US-only story.

---

## 📰 News

- **DeepSeek R1 sparks the open-source reasoning race** — Chinese startup DeepSeek releases a fully open-source reasoning model achieving ~95% of OpenAI o1 performance using pure reinforcement learning, at a fraction of the cost. Signals that US companies may not lead the open-source frontier. *(Jan 2025)*
- **Reasoning models reshape prompting strategies** — With o1 and DeepSeek R1, the field is debating how to interact with chain-of-thought models. o1 costs 12× more than standard LLMs, prompting debate about value vs. capability. AI expert Ben Hylak argues the real shift is in *how* we prompt, not just *what* we prompt. *(Jan 2025)*

---

## 🚀 Model / Product Launches

- **[OpenAI Operator](https://openai.com/operator)** — OpenAI's computer-using agent framework that automates web-based tasks. Positioned as a command center for deploying LLMs across diverse workflows with built-in governance and APIs. Benchmarks show prompt quality still significantly influences output even in agentic settings. *(Jan 2025)*
- **[DeepSeek R1](https://github.com/deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-R1)** — Open-source reasoning model from DeepSeek. Trained with pure reinforcement learning. Matches ~95% of o1 performance. Fully open weights. *(Jan 2025)*

---

## 📄 Research Papers

- **[A Survey on LLM-as-a-Judge](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.15594)** — Gu et al. (16 authors). Comprehensive survey on using LLMs to evaluate other LLMs. Identifies key bias categories: Position Bias (order effects), Verbosity Bias (favoring longer responses), Social Biases (Authority, Bandwagon, Compassion-Fade), Content Biases, and Cognitive Biases. Proposes strategies for consistency and reliability assessment. *(Jan 2025)*
