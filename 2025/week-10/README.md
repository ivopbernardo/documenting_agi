# Week 10, 2025 — Mar 03–07

> **Key Takeaway:** Money and institutions are solidifying behind AI: Anthropic raised $3.5B (valuation $61.5B), OpenAI launched a $50M academic consortium, and the Turing Award went to the fathers of reinforcement learning. AI is no longer just a tech story — it's an infrastructure story. Meanwhile, Mistral quietly shipped a practical OCR tool that will matter far more to enterprise users than another benchmark.

---

## 📰 News

- **OpenAI launches NextGenAI consortium** — $50M initiative with 15 research institutions (Caltech, Harvard, MIT, Oxford, Duke). Covers research grants, compute funding, and API access. Aims to democratize AI across academia: healthcare, digital libraries, AI-powered education. *(Mar 2025)*
- **Anthropic raises $3.5B — valuation hits $61.5B** — Backed by Bessemer Venture Partners, Cisco Investments, D1 Capital Partners. One of the largest AI funding rounds to date. Fuels Claude model development and competition with OpenAI. *(Mar 2025)*
- **Opera integrates AI browsing agent** — AI-powered assistant for smarter tab management, real-time page summaries, and contextual recommendations. Part of the broader trend of browsers becoming AI-augmented personal assistants. *(Mar 2025)*
- **Turing Award goes to Barto & Sutton** — Reinforcement learning pioneers Andrew Barto and Richard Sutton awarded the ACM Turing Award for foundational RL contributions. Their work underpins modern AI from robotics to LLM training and AlphaGo. *(Mar 2025)*

## 🚀 Model / Product Launches

- **[Mistral OCR API](https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-ocr)** — API that converts PDF documents into AI-ready markdown. Targets researchers, legal professionals, and enterprises with large document repositories. Makes structured data extraction from PDFs significantly more efficient. *(Mar 2025)*

## 📄 Research Papers

- **[shapiq: Shapley Interactions for Machine Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.01649)** — Introduces `shapiq`, a Python package for computing Shapley Values and Shapley Interactions across ML models including transformers, XGBoost, and LightGBM. Extends standard SHAP feature attribution to capture interaction effects between features. Key tool for explainable AI practitioners. *(Mar 2025)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- Barto & Sutton's Turing Award is a reminder that the foundations of modern AI (RL, policy gradients, value functions) were laid decades ago — the current moment is engineering scale applied to deep theoretical work.
- Anthropic's $61.5B valuation alongside OpenAI's academic consortium signals a bifurcation: safety-focused labs attracting massive capital while simultaneously trying to broaden access. The tension between those two goals will define the next few years.
- Mistral OCR is a good example of AI value creation happening below the hype line — unglamorous document processing is where enterprises actually spend time.
