## 📰 News

- **Nvidia excludes China from revenue and profit forecasts** — Formal acknowledgment that geopolitical volatility and US export controls make China an unreliable revenue source. $8B impact in Q2. Could trigger similar accounting shifts among other US chipmakers. *(Jun 2025)*
- **OpenAI's open-source model delayed** — The long-teased "open" model (first announced in W14) delayed due to safety evaluations and alignment concerns. Raises questions about whether the risks of open frontier models are still poorly understood. *(Jun 2025)*
- **Glean raises $150M at $7.2B valuation** — AI-powered enterprise work assistant raises nine months after its previous round. Investors treating AI copilots as future workplace staples; enterprise knowledge search/summarization seen as durable category. *(Jun 2025)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- Nvidia excluding China from forecasts is not just a financial disclosure — it's a strategic declaration. The GPU supply chain is now explicitly bifurcating along geopolitical lines.
- OpenAI's open model delay is notable given the competitive pressure from Meta, Mistral, and DeepSeek. Safety concerns may be real, or this may reflect internal disagreement about the strategic wisdom of opening up at all.
- Glean's valuation reflects a durable enterprise thesis: even if foundation models commoditize, the integration layer — connecting AI to a company's specific knowledge base — remains valuable and sticky.
