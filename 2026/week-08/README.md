# Week 08, 2026 — Feb 16–22

> **Key Takeaway:** OpenAI quietly removed the word "safety" from its mission statement while restructuring further toward for-profit — a tell that would become important days later. Meanwhile, Fei-Fei Li's World Labs raised $1B to build spatial AI as an LLM alternative, and Perplexity abandoned its ads model entirely to focus on user trust. The divergence in business model philosophy across labs is becoming a defining feature of the competitive landscape.

---

## 📰 News

- **OpenAI removes "safety" from mission statement** — Updated mission statement as part of further for-profit restructuring. The word "safety" was removed. Described as a "tell" for what would follow in W09. *(Feb 2026)*
- **Perplexity abandons ads model** — Pivoting to business models focused on user trust. Reversal of the ad-supported strategy that was industry-standard. Contrasts sharply with ChatGPT's live ads (W06). *(Feb 2026)*
- **Fei-Fei Li's World Labs raises $1B** — Startup focused on spatial AI as an alternative path to AGI beyond LLMs. Joins LeCun's AMI Labs (W04) and Bezos's Project Prometheus (W47 2025) in the funded "beyond LLMs" bets. *(Feb 2026)*
- **Microsoft gaming leadership: no AI content flooding** — Publicly pledged not to flood the gaming ecosystem with low-quality AI-generated content. Notable given Microsoft's broader AI-everywhere strategy. *(Feb 2026)*

## 💬 Opinion & Analysis

- OpenAI removing "safety" from its mission statement, following the alignment team dissolution (W06), forms a clear directional pattern over three weeks. Each individual change has an explanation; together they describe a deliberate de-emphasis of safety as a primary organisational value.
- Perplexity abandoning ads while OpenAI doubles down on them is the clearest market signal about which model is more confident in enterprise/subscription revenue. Perplexity is betting that trust is more valuable than ad revenue at this stage. That's either prescient or reckless depending on how quickly trust translates to revenue.
- Fei-Fei Li's $1B raise for spatial AI now completes a funded alternative architecture cluster: World Labs (spatial), AMI Labs/LeCun (world models), Project Prometheus/Bezos (physical AI). All three are betting against pure language model scaling. The combined capital behind these bets is now substantial enough that if any one delivers, it changes the trajectory of the field.
